Wickbearers — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 7, 2026 · App: Wickbearers (com.lastwick.last_wick) · Developer contact: anguijm@gmail.com

Wickbearers is a cooperative tile-placement game. It was built to collect as little data as possible. There are no user accounts, no advertising, no analytics, and no sale or sharing of data for marketing of any kind.

Information the app handles

  • Anonymous identifier. When you play online, the app signs in with Firebase Anonymous Authentication, which generates a random identifier for your device. It is not linked to your name, email address, phone number, or any other identity.
  • Display name. The nickname you type is shown to the other players in your private game room and stored with that room's data. It can be anything; it is never verified or linked to a real identity.
  • Game data. Online games store gameplay data (room code, player nicknames, moves, and board state) in Google Cloud Firestore so that the players in a room can share a live game. Rooms are private and joinable only with their invite code.
  • On-device data. Your nickname, last room code, and sound preference are stored only on your device and never uploaded. Solo games run entirely offline on your device.

What the app does NOT do

  • No advertising and no advertising SDKs.
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • No collection of contacts, location, photos, microphone, or files.
  • No sale, rental, or sharing of data with third parties for their own purposes.

Service providers

Online features run on Google Firebase (Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore), acting as a data processor that stores the game data described above on the developer's behalf. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS). See Firebase's privacy documentation.

Data retention and deletion

Game rooms are transient: they exist to run a session among friends. You can stop using the app at any time; on-device data is removed by uninstalling the app or clearing its storage. To request deletion of any room data associated with a room code you played in, email anguijm@gmail.com and it will be deleted.

Children

Wickbearers is not directed at children under 13, and the app collects no personal information beyond what is described above.
